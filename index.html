Gojo Satoru

Domain Expantion

Gojo Satoru’s Domain Expansion - Unlimited Void, traps opponents inside an infinite space of knowledge. Within the domain, the mind is forced to perceive everything at once—sight, sound, thoughts, and sensations endlessly flowing without pause. This overwhelming flood paralyzes the target completely, leaving them conscious but unable to act. Even a fraction of a second inside Unlimited Void is enough to incapacitate ordinary humans. The domain perfectly reflects Gojo’s Limitless technique and Six Eyes, symbolizing absolute control over infinity. Visually, it is depicted as a serene yet terrifying void, where boundless information replaces reality itself. Unlimited Void stands as one of the most refined and deadly domain expansions in the world of jujutsu sorcery. Gojo’s Six Eyes drastically reduce cursed energy consumption, making his techniques almost cost-free. He can keep Infinity active continuously, even while sleeping. Unlimited Void delivers information without meaning, causing mental paralysis. Gojo’s reactions are faster because his brain is constantly optimized by Six Eyes. He intentionally limits himself to avoid accidentally killing allies. The 0.2-second Domain Expansion was designed to protect civilians. His blindfold actually helps control overwhelming visual data. Gojo can instantly analyze enemy techniques upon sight.

Suguru Geto

Cursed Spirit Manipulation

Suguru Geto is a powerful special-grade sorcerer whose innate technique, Cursed Spirit Manipulation, allows him to absorb, control, and command cursed spirits. By exorcising curses and consuming them, Geto can store thousands of spirits within himself and deploy them at will. These controlled curses retain their unique abilities, making his combat style highly versatile and overwhelming. Geto can also combine multiple cursed spirits into devastating attacks or release them strategically to dominate battlefields. This technique grants him immense tactical advantage, enabling large-scale destruction and precise control simultaneously. Cursed Spirit Manipulation reflects Geto’s ideology—viewing non-sorcerers as inferior and believing sorcerers should rule the world. Feared for both his power and conviction, Geto stands as one of the most dangerous curse users in Jujutsu Kaisen. Geto’s technique allows him to reuse cursed spirits indefinitely unless they are destroyed. He can extract techniques from special-grade curses after absorbing them. His body can hold thousands of curses, but it places extreme mental strain. Geto strongly hated meaningless curse exorcism without purpose. He was once Gojo Satoru’s closest friend, not rival. His ideology shift began after witnessing sorcerers die protecting non-sorcerers. Even special-grade sorcerers feared facing him in large-scale battles.

Ryomen Sukuna

King Of Curses

Ryomen Sukuna, known as the King of Curses, is the most feared and powerful cursed spirit in Jujutsu Kaisen. Originating from the golden age of jujutsu, Sukuna was once a human sorcerer whose overwhelming strength and cruelty transcended death. After his defeat, his power was sealed within twenty indestructible fingers, each containing immense cursed energy. Sukuna is ruthless, arrogant, and sadistic, viewing both humans and curses as inferior beings. His combat abilities are terrifying, combining unmatched physical strength, mastery over cursed techniques, and devastating domain expansion, Malevolent Shrine. Unlike most domains, it manifests without a barrier, allowing indiscriminate destruction over a wide area. Sukuna represents pure chaos and domination, standing as the ultimate embodiment of fear and absolute power in the world of curses. Sukuna was originally a human sorcerer, not a natural cursed spirit. His four arms and two faces symbolize divine wrath and dominance. Sukuna’s cursed energy control is extremely refined, not just overwhelming. Malevolent Shrine lacks a barrier, breaking standard domain rules. He can adjust slashing attacks (Cleave & Dismantle) based on enemy durability. Sukuna understands cursed techniques instinctively, even when borrowed. He heals effortlessly using advanced Reverse Cursed Technique.

Toji Fushiguro

The Sorcerer Killer

Toji Fushiguro, known as The Sorcerer Killer, is a legendary assassin feared even among elite jujutsu sorcerers. Born into the Zenin clan without cursed energy, Toji possesses a rare Heavenly Restriction, granting him superhuman physical strength, speed, and heightened senses. Free from cursed energy, he cannot be easily detected, making him a perfect predator of sorcerers. Toji relies on cursed tools rather than techniques, wielding them with deadly precision and experience. Calm, ruthless, and highly strategic, he approaches every battle as a professional contract. His ability to overwhelm powerful sorcerers through pure skill and instinct proves that strength does not rely solely on cursed energy. Toji embodies raw combat mastery, earning his infamous title through countless slain sorcerers. Born with zero cursed energy, making him completely invisible to sorcerer detection. His Heavenly Restriction grants extreme strength, speed, reflexes, and durability beyond most sorcerers. Possesses instinct-level combat sense, allowing him to react faster than cursed techniques. Can defeat powerful sorcerers without using domains or cursed techniques. Stores weapons inside a cursed spirit, bypassing detection and surprise attacks. Fought Gojo Satoru twice and nearly killed him through strategy alone. Immune to many curse-based effects due to lack of cursed energy.

Yuta Okkotsu

Reverse Curse Technique

Yuta Okkotsu is one of the strongest modern jujutsu sorcerers, renowned for his exceptional mastery of Reverse Cursed Technique. Unlike standard cursed energy, which is fueled by negative emotions, Reverse Cursed Technique is created by multiplying cursed energy against itself, producing positive energy capable of healing. Yuta can rapidly heal severe injuries, restore others, and sustain himself during prolonged battles. His immense cursed energy reserves allow him to use this technique far more efficiently than most sorcerers. Combined with his bond to Rika, Yuta’s control over Reverse Cursed Technique makes him both a devastating fighter and a powerful support ally. This rare ability highlights Yuta’s balanced strength—capable of destruction and restoration—marking him as a uniquely versatile and dangerous sorcerer. Yuta can output Reverse Cursed Technique, allowing him to heal others, not just himself. His cursed energy reserves are so vast that even Gojo compares them to Sukuna’s level. Rika acts as an external cursed energy storage, amplifying Yuta’s abilities. Yuta can copy cursed techniques, but only under specific conditions. Despite his strength, Yuta has a gentle personality, which contrasts his destructive potential. He was considered a special-grade sorcerer before fully understanding his powers.
